Notes:

Styling on this page has been designed with the consideration that the target audience is adults aged 30-75. This is an age group where people are more likely to have the financial means to buy and sell items at auction. The colors used are subtle, yet visible to all users. The contrast of the colors has been tested through accessibility programs and meets the necessary standards. The images and buttons have small hover effects that make the site more interactive, without being too flashy.
